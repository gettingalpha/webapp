FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is AlertX and how does it work?

AlertX is our AI-powered stock alert system that monitors market movements, corporate announcements, news, and social media to deliver highly relevant, timely notifications about the stocks you follow. It uses advanced algorithms to filter out noise and deliver only actionable insights.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to additional features, and when downgrading, your current plan remains active until the end of the billing cycle.

Is my financial data secure with Getting Alpha?

Absolutely. We use bank-grade encryption to protect your data. We never share your personal or financial information with third parties, and our systems are regularly audited for security compliance. Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.

Do you provide investment advice?

Getting Alpha provides information and tools to help you make informed investment decisions, but we do not provide personalized investment advice. Our Alpha Pro plan includes sessions with financial advisors who can offer guidance, but you should always consult with a qualified financial professional before making investment decisions.
